Disaster Safety Guides

Flood Safety

  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Disconnect electrical appliances.
  • Do not walk through moving water.
  • Do not drive into flooded areas.
Warning: Just 6 inches of moving water can knock an adult down.

Earthquake

  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On.
  • Stay away from glass and windows.
  • Do not use elevators.
  • Do not run outside while shaking.
Be prepared for aftershocks that can occur minutes or days later.

Fire Safety

  • Crawl low under smoke to exit.
  • Use stairs, avoid elevators.
  • Stop, Drop, and Roll if clothes catch fire.
  • Never open a warm door.

Cyclone / Hurricane

  • Board up windows or use shutters.
  • Stay indoors away from windows.
  • Keep emergency radio battery charged.
  • Do not go outside during the "eye".

Extreme Heat

  • Stay hydrated, drink plenty of water.
  • Wear light, loose-fitting clothing.
  • Avoid strenuous activity midday.
  • Never leave pets/kids in parked cars.

Landslide

  • Listen for unusual sounds like trees cracking.
  • Move away from the path of debris.
  • Curl into a ball to protect your head.
